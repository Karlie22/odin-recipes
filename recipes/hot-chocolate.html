Hot Chocolate

Description

Hot chocolate is the perfect cozy drink for winter's cold days. This recipe will show you how to make a perfect italian hot chocolate. What is the difference between a regular hot chocolate and an italian one you ask? The italian hot chocolate is thick and creamy and it is usually eaten with a teaspoon. The best part is the whipped cream on top. You are going to love this!

Ingredients

  • Cocoa powder
  • Cornstarch
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla sirup
  • Milk
  • whipped cream

Steps

  1. In a pot mix the cocoa powder, sugar and cornstarch;
  2. Add a little bit of milk, the vanilla syrup and mix well (to prevent clumps);
  3. Add all the milk and put in on the stove on low-medium heat and stir often;
  4. When it stars to bubble, stir without stopping while it boils for four minutes;
  5. Now serve it in a cup and put all the whipped cream your heart desires and done!
